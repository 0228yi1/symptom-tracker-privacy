Privacy Policy – Symptom Tracker

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Symptom Tracker respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Health-related information entered by the user (such as symptoms and notes)
  • Dates and times associated with entries

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core functionality of the app
  • To display symptom history and trends

3. Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on the user’s device
  • We do not upload data to external servers

4. Data Sharing

  • We do not share personal or health data with third parties
  • No data is sold or used for advertising

5. Data Security

  • Reasonable technical measures are used to protect stored data
  • However, no method of storage is 100% secure

6. Children’s Privacy

  • This app is not intended for children under the age of 13

7. User Rights

  • Users can delete their data at any time by clearing app data
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored information

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • This policy may be updated from time to time
  • Any changes will be posted on this page
